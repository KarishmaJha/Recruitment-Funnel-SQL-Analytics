# Recruitment Funnel & Hiring Performance Insights

## 1. Candidate Source Effectiveness
- Indeed and LinkedIn generated the highest number of candidates.
- Referral-based candidates showed higher conversion to offer and joining stages.

## 2. Recruitment Funnel Bottlenecks
- The Interview stage had the highest candidate drop-off.
- Indicates a need for better candidate screening and alignment before interviews.

## 3. Recruiter Performance
- Recruiters handling healthcare roles showed higher joining success.
- Balanced workload among recruiters could improve overall hiring efficiency.

## 4. Time-to-Hire Analysis
- Average time-to-hire was within acceptable range for contract healthcare roles.
- Faster hiring observed for high-demand roles such as ICU and Med-Surg nurses.

## 5. Offer to Joining Ratio
- Majority of issued offers converted into successful joinings.
- Competitive pay rates positively impacted joining decisions.

## 6. Skill Demand Trends
- ICU and Med-Surg nursing skills were in highest demand.
- Data supports prioritizing sourcing efforts for these skills.
